Refund policy

Clear cancellation and refund guidance for the current Solvoriz product stage

This page explains how free student access works today and how future recruiter billing would be handled if paid plans are introduced.

Student accounts

Student builder accounts are currently free to use. There is no subscription fee for creating a profile, showcasing projects, sharing GitHub links, or participating in the builder community.

Recruiter billing and future paid plans

Recruiter access may be beta, custom, or invite-based. If Solvoriz introduces a paid recruiter plan later, the pricing, billing cycle, renewal terms, and cancellation rules will be shown clearly before checkout or activation.

Cancellation process

If a future recruiter plan includes recurring billing, cancellation instructions will be available from the account area or through support. Cancellation timing and access period details will be stated in the plan terms.

Refund handling

Any future paid recruiter plan will include a clearly stated refund policy. Refund eligibility, time windows, and exceptions will be explained before payment so users know the billing terms in advance.

Billing support contact

For billing questions, cancellation help, or payment disputes related to any future recruiter plan, contact support@solvoriz.com.
