FAQ

Common questions about Solvoriz

Clear answers for builders, recruiters, and anyone evaluating how the platform works.

Solvoriz is a project-first builder network where student creators showcase real work, GitHub repos, live demos, skills, and availability so recruiters can find and contact them through projects instead of resumes.
Student accounts are free. Recruiter access may be custom, beta, or invite-based during early rollout, and paid terms will be shown clearly if billing is introduced.
Recruiters search student builders by skills, project portfolios, availability, and other profile signals, then shortlist strong matches and start direct chats inside the platform.
Solvoriz focuses on proof of work. Instead of evaluating students only through resumes, recruiters can review what they have built, shipped, and linked through demos and GitHub.
Students create profiles around projects, skills, demos, and repositories. Recruiters evaluate that evidence directly, which creates a hiring flow based on real output rather than resume wording alone.
Support details are available on the Support page, including contact categories, expected response times, and a support email placeholder for platform and billing questions.
Students use Solvoriz for free. Recruiter pricing may be custom or invite-based during beta, and any future paid plan will display pricing and billing terms up front.
There are currently no student fees. If paid recruiter plans are introduced, cancellation timing, billing terms, and refund handling will be stated clearly, and billing issues can be routed through support.
